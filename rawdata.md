### Service Descriptions (All 8 Services)

**Project Management**
End-to-end command of your project's scope, schedule, cost, and quality from inception to handover. We replace fragmented coordination with a single line of accountability, eliminating the gaps where budget overruns and schedule slippage typically take root.

**Construction Management**
Disciplined oversight of the entire construction phase, integrating contractors, consultants, and stakeholders under one governance structure. This mitigates the risk of misaligned execution, contractor disputes, and avoidable rework that compromise both cost and delivery dates.

**Site Supervision**
On-the-ground technical vigilance ensuring every element built matches design intent, specification, and code. We stop quality failures, non-compliant work, and hidden defects at the source—before they become costly post-completion liabilities.

**Quantity Surveying**
Forensic-level cost measurement, valuation, and commercial control across every project stage. Our QS function exposes pricing irregularities, prevents overpayment, and gives developers verifiable confidence that every baht spent reflects true value delivered.

**Project Audit**
Independent, evidence-based validation of project performance, compliance, and financial integrity. This is the assurance layer that confirms what was promised has actually been delivered—closing the accountability loop that most developers discover too late is missing.

**Client's Representatives**
We act as the developer's authoritative voice on-site and at the table, defending your interests across every contractor, consultant, and authority interaction. This eliminates information asymmetry and ensures decisions are made in your favor, not against it.

**Contract and Commercial Management**
Strategic structuring, administration, and enforcement of contracts to protect the developer's commercial position throughout the project. We neutralize the contractual ambiguities and claim exposures that quietly drain budgets and trigger disputes.

**Design and Build**
Integrated delivery combining design leadership and construction execution under unified accountability. This model collapses the traditional gaps between designer and builder, eliminating scope disputes, coordination failures, and the cost escalation that arises when responsibility is divided.

### Responsive Strategy
- Desktop (1440px+): Full multi-column layouts
- Tablet (768px): 2-column max, condensed nav

### Interaction Patterns
- Button hover: lift 2px, arrow slides right
- Card hover: lift 4-8px, gold border/underline
- Form focus: bottom border draws gold, label floats
- Scroll reveals: fade-up 0.6s with 24px translation
- Numbers count-up when entering viewport
- Smooth page transitions with thin gold progress bar
