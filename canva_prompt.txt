## DESIGN INSTRUCTIONS FOR CANVA

**For Each Project Slide:**
1. Dark navy/charcoal background (hex: #1a1a2e or similar)
2. Gold accent line or border (hex: #d4af37 or similar)
3. Place hero image prominently (top 60% of slide, full width or centered large)
4. Display 5 supporting images in a 2-3 grid layout below hero image
5. Include text information (name, category, year, description) with gold highlights for key terms
6. Use cream/white text (#f5f5f5) on dark background for readability
7. Maintain luxury aesthetic with clean spacing and elegant typography

**Overall Presentation Structure:**
1. Title slide with company name and presentation title
2. One slide per project (as detailed above)
3. Team members page (optional: with member photos, names, roles)
4. Closing slide with company contact information

**Color Scheme Example:**
- Background: #1a1a2e (Dark Navy)
- Accents: #d4af37 (Gold)
- Text: #f5f5f5 (Cream White)
- Highlights: #b8922d (Darker Gold)
